Dai Huen Jai (THC)

Minions: Over 50,000
Headquarters: Hong Kong, China


This villainous gang of mystery men is one of the most sinister of the Triads involved in extensive Drug trafficking, loan-sharking, fraud, counterfeiting, prostitution and human trafficking all over the world. They were supposedly former Red Guards to the deceased leader Mao Zedong and originated during the Chinese Communist Revolution of 1949 as the Big Circle Gang (the destination given to the soldier class members in the group).The highest ranked equivocates to a Commanding Officer or Commissar and are generally involved in legitimate business, gambling, and in the position of Lutsze-yeh (a managing or controlling lawyer's clerk).
